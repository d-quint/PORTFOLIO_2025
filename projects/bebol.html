Bebol's World Champion SIKAPTala 2025

A child-friendly gamified learning app democratizing speech therapy for Filipino children through AI-powered voice analysis, interactive scenarios, and offline accessibility.

Completed: January 2025
Category: Educational Tech

Overview

The Philippines faces a critical speech therapy crisis: with only around 400 registered Speech-Language Pathologists nationwide and 60% concentrated in Metro Manila, rural provinces face a staggering ratio of 1 SLP for every 8,108 clients. For the estimated 1 in 7 Filipino children living with speech disorders like stuttering, articulation errors, or phonological delays, this scarcity means many grow up without the tools to speak, learn, or connect—deepening cycles of exclusion in education and society.

Bebol's World emerged from a 24-hour hackathon challenge to address this generational emergency. Our team designed a comprehensive mobile app concept that transforms speech therapy from an inaccessible privilege into an engaging, gamified experience. Through AI-powered voice analysis, interactive role-playing scenarios, and offline-first functionality, Bebol's World brings evidence-based speech interventions to children in remote barangays with no local SLPs, families who cannot afford ₱1,500-₱3,000 per therapy session, and overcrowded public schools lacking special education resources.

Created entirely in Figma during the SIKAPTala 2025 hackathon, our no-code prototype won the championship by demonstrating how technology can bridge geographic and socioeconomic divides. The app features a child-friendly mascot named "Bebol" who guides learners through structured modules—from basic phonics to real-life dialogue simulations—while parent dashboards provide actionable progress insights. By combining clinical best practices with inclusive design, Bebol's World represents a scalable solution to ensure early intervention is no longer a privilege but a possibility for all Filipino children.

Key Features

AI-Powered Voice Recording & Analysis

Real-time pronunciation evaluation system that detects syllable emphasis errors, articulation mistakes, and voice projection issues. Children receive immediate visual and auditory feedback through animated guides demonstrating proper tongue placement, with progress scores to track improvement over time.

Structured Gamified Learning Path

Candy Crush-style progression system with multi-sensory content including phonics games, interactive flashcards, and vocabulary builders. Adaptive difficulty adjusts challenges based on performance, with rewards like badges and in-app currency to unlock avatars and story scenarios, keeping children engaged through short attention spans.

Immersive Real-Life Dialogue Simulations

Low-pressure role-playing environments where children practice everyday conversations like ordering pan de sal at a virtual bakery, greeting neighbors with "Kumusta?", or asking directions to a sari-sari store. AI characters respond based on pronunciation accuracy, building confidence and reducing communication anxiety.

Comprehensive Parent Support System

Secure linked parent-child accounts with real-time analytics tracking articulation milestones, weekly progress summaries, and custom reports highlighting strengths and areas needing practice. Includes an integrated forum where parents can connect with SLPs and other caregivers for tailored advice and community support.

Offline-First Accessibility

Downloadable learning modules ensure continuity in regions with unstable internet connectivity, critical during typhoon outages or for families in remote barangays. Children can practice on budget smartphones during power interruptions, with progress syncing automatically when connection is restored.

Multi-Modal Inclusive Interaction

Supports touch, voice, and icon-based inputs to accommodate diverse needs. Nonverbal children can drag icons to communicate basic requests, motor-impaired users leverage voice commands, and adaptive learning paths offer simplified drills for severe impediments with visual mouth guides for apraxia learners.

Impact & Results

Bebol's World addresses a critical gap in Philippine healthcare and education, targeting children aged 4-12 who face systemic exclusion from speech therapy due to geography, poverty, and resource scarcity. Our solution extends beyond clinical settings to empower families, educators, and communities.
